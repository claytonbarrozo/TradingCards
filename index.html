Golden Retriever

Golden Retrievers are very smart dogs that are loyal and friendly to their caregivers.

  • Color: any shades of gold or cream
  • Coat: straight or moderately wavy
  • Lifespan: 10-12 years
  • Origin: Scotland

Golden Retrievers have an instinctive love of water, and are easy to train to basic or advanced obedience standards. They are well suited to residency in suburban or country environments. Although they need substantial outdoor exercise, they should be housed in a fenced area because of their instinctual tendency to roam.
